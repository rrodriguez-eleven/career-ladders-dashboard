How to use this dashboard

Browse by Department

Pick a department from the sidebar to see its job families and every band, junior to senior. Click any role card to see its full profile: scope, complexity, autonomy, influence, who's currently in it, and the formal job description if one's on file.

Directory

Search everyone at Eleven by name, or browse every defined role grouped by status: current openings, filled roles, vacant roles, and future growth levels.

Levels

See the same band sliced across every department at once, useful for understanding what "Band 3" or "Director" tends to mean company-wide, not just in your own team.

Job Descriptions

The full library of formal job descriptions on file, searchable in one place.

Compare

Add two roles (from any card's "+" button) to see them side by side, then open the full analysis for Growth Areas or Key Differences, Day-to-Day Differences, Main Takeaways, and a few concrete things Worth Exploring. Treat this as a conversation-starter with your manager, not an official growth plan.

Search

The search bar at the top of the sidebar jumps straight to any role or person by name, from anywhere in the app.

See something that looks off?

Every role and comparison has a small "Something look off?" link at the bottom. Click it, describe what's wrong, and it goes straight to the People Team.
